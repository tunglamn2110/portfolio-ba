AROMA Spa Management System

Optimizing spa operations to reduce no-show rates and streamline resource allocation using data-driven scheduling.

Project Overview

AROMA Spa is a premium wellness center facing operational challenges due to manual booking processes. As the Lead Business Analyst, I was responsible for eliciting requirements, analyzing current state processes (As-Is), and proposing a centralized management system (To-Be) to improve efficiency.

My Role
Lead BA Intern
Timeline
Jan 2026 - Present

The Problem

  • High No-show Rate: 26.13% of appointments were cancelled or no-shows, leading to wasted therapist hours.
  • Revenue Loss: Estimated ~15% drop in Q4 2025 revenue directly correlated to scheduling inefficiencies.
  • Decentralized Data: Lack of a unified system caused booking conflicts between branches and service rooms.

The Process (Solution)

Requirement Elicitation

Conducted interviews with Branch Managers and Therapists. Analyzed 3 months of booking logs (Excel) to identify root causes of scheduling conflicts.

Process Modeling (BPMN)

Mapped the "As-Is" process to highlight bottlenecks. Designed the "To-Be" process flow focusing on automated slot verification.

[Image of BPMN Process Flow Diagram]

Solution Documentation (BRD)

Wrote a comprehensive Business Requirement Document (BRD) including Functional/Non-functional requirements and Use Case specifications.
